Our Himalayan Story

Manali Outdoor Retreat by Yatraverse is a sanctuary where rugged Himalayan beauty meets refined hospitality. Located in the serene village of Burwa on Shanag Road, we offer an escape from the tourist bustle. Surrounded by lush apple orchards and snow-capped peaks, our retreat blends traditional wood architecture with modern luxury.

Lush Lawns & Gardens

Our manicured gardens feature seasonal blooms like tulips—the perfect spot for morning yoga or evening tea under the stars.

Panoramic Balconies

Every room is designed to bring the mountains inside. Enjoy 360-degree views of the Dhauladhar range from your private deck.

The Retreat Gallery

Explore the Valley

Stay in Burwa to bypass Manali traffic and reach these iconic and hidden gems faster.

3 KM

Solang Valley

The adventure hub of Himachal. Famous for Paragliding, Zorbing, and the ropeway to Mt. Phatru.

16 KM

Atal Tunnel & Sissu

Drive through the world's longest tunnel to witness the golden landscapes and waterfalls of Lahaul Valley.

4 KM

Anjani Mahadev

A hidden spiritual trek from Solang leading to a massive natural ice Shivalinga and a 100ft waterfall.

OFFBEAT

Sethan (Igloo Village)

15 km away. A restricted Buddhist hamlet offering the best stargazing and winter Igloo stays in India.

OFFBEAT

Jana Waterfall

35 km away. A scenic drive to a local heritage spot famous for traditional 'Siddu' and mountain thalis.

OFFBEAT

Sajla Waterfalls

12 km away. A peaceful pine forest walk leading to a hidden pool—perfect for a private nature picnic.

Day Trip: Tirthan Valley & Jalori Pass

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage 'Great Himalayan National Park' (90km). Trek to the sacred Serolsar Lake or enjoy trout fishing in the pristine Tirthan river.
